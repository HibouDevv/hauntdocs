## Unable to login?

<Steps>
    <Step title="Check Your Credentials">
        Please ensure you are using the **correct** Email & Password when logging in!
    </Step>
    <Step title="Reset Your Password">
        Forgot your password? Don't worry! Hit `Forgot Password` on the [Login page](https://haunt.gg/login) to reset it!
    </Step>
    <Step title="Discord Login Issues">
        If you login with **Discord**, ensure you're using the **correct** discord account!
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
    Still unable to login? Open a support ticket in our Discord server!
</Warning>
